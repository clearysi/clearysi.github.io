What we provide

ELIXIR Ireland offers training in fundamental data science skills for life scientist such as R analysis as well as data management planning. Our training primarily, to date, has focused on offering training courses to over 100 PhD students within the Science Foundation Ireland (SFI) Centre for Research Training in Genomics Data Science program.
